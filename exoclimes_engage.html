Exoclimes Engage

Engaging the public with Exoclimes
We're working with Exeter Science Centre to organise a relaxed event on the final afternoon of Exoclimes, to showcase the amazing range of research from the conference!
The event will take place from 2-5pm on Friday 30th June at Exeter Guildhall shopping Centrein the city centre,
and contributors can work with Exeter Science Centre's team to prepare a short, very basic, image-only (or image-free) talk, with or without props.

Keen to take part? Contact Natalie & Alice (researchers@exetersciencecentre.org) Exeter Science Centre by Monday 12th June!
