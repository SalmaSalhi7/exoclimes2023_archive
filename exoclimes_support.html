Exoclimes Support

At Exoclimes we will be holding two sessions aimed at co-developing a Mental Health Toolkit, which will be freely available to researchers. These two sessions will be separated into one aimed at people who are supporting teams of researchers and others which are, perhaps, at earlier stages of their careers. Each session will be 1 hour, and will be chaired by a professional, external provider, Katie Nicoll-Baines (University of Edinburgh). Each session will be limited to 25 people, in order to create an effective session.
