Registration

Exoclimes VI has capacity for 200 delegates, 50 of which can also be accommodated on the ExoSLAM summer school. There is a two stage process, first an application or pre-registration (which will include estimated costs to inform your decision), followed by registration and booking, the links for which will appear on this page.
